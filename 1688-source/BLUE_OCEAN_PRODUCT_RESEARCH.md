## 3. Product Research Methodology

### Step 1: Identify Candidate Niches

**Demand Signal Analysis:**
- Use Google Trends to check keyword trajectory over 12 months — only pursue upward or stable trends
- Check AliExpress Dropshipping Center for real-time sales data
- Use TikTok/Instagram search to spot products going viral before they saturate AliExpress

**Competition Ratio Calculation:**
The core metric is **search demand / number of active sellers**:

```
Competition Score = (Monthly search volume on AliExpress) / (Number of sellers with >10 orders)

Blue Ocean:  Score > 500  (high demand, few sellers)
Contested:   Score 100-500 (moderate competition)
Red Ocean:   Score < 100  (saturated)
```

**Quick Competition Check on AliExpress:**
1. Search the product keyword on AliExpress
2. Note total results count (number of competing listings)
3. Sort by "Orders" — count how many listings have >100 orders in 30 days
4. If <20 listings have >100 monthly orders but search volume is high = blue ocean signal

**CRITICAL: Category Validation**
Before adding any product to the sourcing pipeline, verify it maps to a leaf category in `aliexpress/STORE_CATEGORIES.md` or `aliexpress/data/aliexpress-categories.json`. Products without a matching authorized category WILL BE REJECTED during publishing.

### Step 2: Validate Product Viability

**Minimum Criteria Checklist:**
- [ ] Wholesale price on 1688: CNY 37-500 (retail CNY 74-1,000 at 2x)
- [ ] NOT a branded product (check against `excludeBrands` in `config.ts`)
- [ ] Maps to an authorized leaf category in this store
- [ ] Weight under 2kg (shipping cost control)
- [ ] Not fragile / low return rate potential
- [ ] AliExpress rating >= 4.5 stars for similar products
- [ ] At least 100+ reviews on existing similar listings
- [ ] Growth velocity: daily review count increasing over past 30 days

**Margin Calculation:**
```
1688 wholesale price:           CNY XX
x 2 (markup):                   CNY XX
- AliExpress commission (~8%):  CNY XX
- Estimated shipping/packaging: CNY XX
= Net profit per unit:          CNY XX
Target: Net profit >= 30% of retail price
```

### Step 3: Source on 1688

**Search Strategy:**
1. Use the Chinese keywords from Section 2
2. Append "跨境专供" (cross-border) or "一件代发" (dropshipping) modifiers
3. Filter results:
   - Sort by "销量" (sales volume)
   - Price range: CNY 37-500
   - Supplier type: "实力商家" (verified merchant) or "超级工厂" (super factory)
   - Minimum 3 years on platform ("诚信通 >= 3年")

### Step 4: Test Before Scaling

1. Order 2-3 samples from different suppliers
2. Evaluate: build quality, packaging, actual vs. listed specs
3. Check if product matches listing photos
4. Test shipping time to target market
5. Only proceed with bulk/listing if sample passes all checks

## 4. Supplier Evaluation on 1688

### Red Flags to Avoid
- New stores (< 1 year on platform)
- No transaction history or very low volume
- DSR score below 4.5 in any category
- No certifications for electronics products
- Unusually low prices compared to category average (quality risk)
- Won't send samples
- Copied/stolen product images (reverse image search to verify)

### Green Flags to Prioritize
- "超级工厂" (Super Factory) badge
- "实力商家" (Verified Merchant) badge
- DSR scores >= 4.8 across all three categories
- Supports "一件代发" (single-piece dropshipping)
- Has "跨境专供" (cross-border supply) designation
- Provides CE/FCC/RoHS certificates
- Willing to customize packaging (OEM capability)
- Active "生意参谋" (business advisor) data showing consistent sales

## 5. Ongoing Research System

### Weekly Routine (30 minutes)

1. **Google Trends Check** (5 min)
   - Monitor 5-10 keyword terms from your niche watchlist
   - Flag any keyword with >20% growth week-over-week

2. **AliExpress Dropshipping Center** (10 min)
   - Check "Hot Products" -> filter by your authorized categories
   - Look for products with high orders but low review count (new demand)
   - Note products with rising order velocity in past 7 days

3. **1688 New Products Scan** (10 min)
   - Search your core categories with "新品" (new product) modifier
   - Sort by "上架时间" (listing date) to see latest additions
   - Look for products listed in past 30 days with >= 100 sales

4. **Social Media Trend Scan** (5 min)
   - Check TikTok hashtags: #tiktokmademebuyit, #amazonfinds, #aliexpressfinds
   - Note any electronics/gadgets trending that aren't yet saturated on AliExpress

### Monthly Deep Dive (2 hours)

1. **Competition Audit**
   - For each listed product, re-check the competition ratio
   - If ratio drops below 200, consider price/listing optimization or pivoting

2. **New Niche Exploration**
   - Products with Orders Last 30 Days > 200, Total Reviews < 100 (high demand, new market)
   - Products with 7-Day Order Growth > 100% (explosive growth)
   - Products with Star Rating < 3.7, Orders 30d > 200 (opportunity to offer better quality)

3. **Supplier Relationship Review**
   - Check if current 1688 suppliers have new products
   - Negotiate better prices if volume has increased
   - Evaluate alternative suppliers for best-selling items

4. **Pricing & Margin Review**
   - Recalculate margins accounting for shipping cost changes
   - Adjust retail prices if competitive landscape has shifted
