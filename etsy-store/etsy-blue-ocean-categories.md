## Strategy Summary

Etsy buyers pay premium prices for items that feel **unique, artisanal, or gift-worthy**. Our approach: source quality products from 1688, optimize copy for Etsy SEO (lifestyle storytelling, 13 tags, keyword-rich titles), and position as curated boutique selections rather than commodity goods.

Unlike AliExpress (price competition), Etsy rewards **perceived uniqueness, emotional appeal, and gift positioning**. The same 1688 product can command 2-3x the AliExpress price on Etsy with proper presentation.

## 🎨 Store Style Direction — MANDATORY (2026-03-28)

**Our Etsy niche: Retro, Vintage-Inspired & Punk/Gothic Aesthetic**

This store curates products with a distinct visual identity — NOT generic commodity accessories. Every product sourced for Etsy should match at least one of these style pillars:

### Sourcing Rules

1. **Style filter is MANDATORY** — when running Task 1 discovery for Etsy, append style keywords to search terms (e.g., "复古项链" not just "项链")
2. **Plain/generic products should be SKIPPED** — a basic black wallet is not interesting; a vintage-style leather wallet with brass buckle IS
3. **Visual appeal > utility** — Etsy buyers shop with their eyes. If a product doesn't photograph well or have visual character, skip it
4. **Gift-worthy test** — Would someone buy this as a unique gift? If yes, list it. If it feels like a Walmart commodity, skip it
5. **Curate aggressively** — Better to have 50 stunning products than 500 generic ones. Quality over quantity on Etsy

### What NOT to Source for Etsy

- Plain solid-color accessories with no design character
- Generic phone cases, basic hair ties, commodity shoe insoles
- Products that look mass-produced without any aesthetic angle
- Anything that could be found cheaper on Amazon/Temu/Shein with identical appearance

### How This Affects the Pipeline

- **Task 1 (Discovery)**: Use style-specific search terms from the table above. Combine category + style (e.g., "复古皮带", "朋克戒指", "哥特耳环")
- **Import to Etsy**: When importing from `1688_source`, prefer products whose titles/images match our style pillars
- **Listing Copy**: Titles and descriptions should emphasize the aesthetic ("Victorian-Inspired Brass Gear Pendant" not "Metal Necklace")
- **Tags**: Include style-specific tags (vintage, retro, gothic, punk, steampunk, bohemian, artisan)

## Cross-Platform Rules

- A product CAN be listed on both AliExpress AND Etsy (different platforms = OK)
- A product CANNOT be on two Etsy stores (ONE STORE PER PLATFORM rule)
- Import to `etsy_autostore` is **non-destructive** — does not change status in `1688_source`
- All brand safety rules apply: `authorized_products` JOIN + `isBannedBrand()` defense

## Etsy SEO Best Practices

1. **Titles**: Max 140 chars, front-load keywords, lifestyle angle ("Dainty Gold Necklace - Everyday Layering Jewelry - Gift for Her")
2. **Tags**: Exactly 13 per listing, long-tail multi-word phrases (not single words)
3. **Descriptions**: Lifestyle storytelling > spec dumps. Include: About, Specs, Perfect For, Care, Shipping sections
4. **Images**: Lifestyle/context photos first, clean product shots after. Min 5, max 10 images.
5. **Pricing**: Include Etsy fees in markup. Target 2.5x 1688 cost = healthy margin after fees.
